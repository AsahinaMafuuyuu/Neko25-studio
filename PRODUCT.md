# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Kravix AI Studio serves creators and product teams building AI video, avatar, voice, and media workflows. They arrive from a multilingual public entry page and move into an authenticated studio where they manage generation tasks, assets, billing, and account settings.

## Product Purpose

The product gives teams one focused workspace for production AI creation: authentication, project entry points, video agent workflows, AI avatars, voice cloning, and a reusable media library. Success looks like a user quickly understanding what the studio can do, trusting the platform enough to sign in, and finding the right creation workflow without friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, precise. The interface should feel like a dependable creative command center: polished enough for customer-facing work, quiet enough for daily production, and clear about what action comes next.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS tropes: oversized decorative gradients, repeated icon-card grids, vague "all-in-one AI" copy, fake metrics without context, and motion that makes a task surface feel like a landing-page animation. Avoid heavy dark-only dashboard styling that hides content or makes media hard to inspect.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with real workflows and media, not abstractions.
2. Keep navigation and calls to action obvious across languages and screen sizes.
3. Use restrained product styling so generation content remains the focus.
4. Make trust signals concrete: authentication, protected routes, storage, and backend readiness.
5. Preserve consistent component vocabulary between the public entry page and the authenticated dashboard.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Keep keyboard focus visible, touch targets comfortable, and motion non-essential with a reduced-motion path. Design copy and layout to tolerate English, Chinese, and Japanese string length changes.
