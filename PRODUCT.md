# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Kravix AI Studio is for independent creators and small content teams producing short videos, short dramas, and social-media-ready content. They arrive with a script, prompt, voice, avatar, or raw material and need to move quickly from idea to a publishable subtitled video.

## Product Purpose

Kravix exists to compress the short-drama production workflow into one focused creator studio: script generation, digital avatars, voice cloning, subtitles, media assets, preview, rendering, and library management. Success means users can turn creative material into polished output with minimal friction, clear progress, and confidence that the result is ready to publish.

## Brand Personality

Premium, Artistic, Fluid. The interface should feel elevated and contemporary without becoming visually tiring. It should carry sharp black-and-white contrast, modern-art restraint, and silky transitions that support momentum rather than calling attention to themselves.

## Anti-references

- Not CapCut's dense, cluttered mobile editing UI.
- Not Canva's overly playful, mass-market flat design style.
- Not gamer, neon, or cyberpunk.
- Not generic cookie-cutter SaaS dashboards, including early Notion-like or Jira-style surfaces.
- Not a cheap AI product with excessive gradients, glow effects, and decorative spectacle layered together.

## Design Principles

- Protect creator momentum: every screen should make the next production step obvious and fast.
- Feel premium through restraint: use contrast, composition, spacing, and material discipline before decoration.
- Keep the workspace calm under heavy creative state: complex generation jobs, assets, and previews should feel organized, not noisy.
- Let motion explain state: transitions should feel fluid and responsive, especially during route changes, generation progress, previews, and handoffs.
- Make artistry operational: the product should feel visually sophisticated while keeping standard controls, labels, and workflows familiar.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG AA as the default baseline. Maintain strong text contrast, color-blind-safe state communication, visible focus states, keyboard-accessible controls, and reduced-motion alternatives for interface transitions. Additional requirements can be revisited in a later iteration.
